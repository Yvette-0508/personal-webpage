Community Reflections on LLM-Powered Financial Research Tools

December 18, 2025 AI Finance Research

I run a traders community with 1,000+ followers, so I actively collect feedback from real user needs and day-to-day workflows. Some issues are being noted and being addressed:

👤 User 1

  1. Hard to verify results: especially for granular data, it’s difficult to tell whether numbers come from reputable sources or if the model is extrapolating/guessing.
  2. Too few visuals: outputs are mostly dense text. Even when charts are requested in the prompt, the generated figures often don’t match what’s needed.
  3. Backtesting doesn’t follow intent: a basic backtest feature struggled to understand prompts—even simple ones—and the results were hard to validate.

General speaking, there still isn’t a strong human AI trust mechanism. Users spend significant time tracing whether underlying data is credible, and that verification cost is still too high.
