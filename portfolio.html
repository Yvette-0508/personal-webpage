Agentic Quantamental Research & Trading Platform

Zen Trading • Oct 2024 - Present

Core Components

Agentic Equity Research — Multi-Hop Reasoning

An agentic equity-research platform (FastAPI + Next.js) where an LLM agent generates per-company multi-hop reasoning theses — tracing how one company's situation propagates across its value chain, upstream to suppliers and downstream to customers — grounded on point-in-time regulatory financials and an editable value-chain context. Every generation is routed through an observability and audit layer (Langfuse plus a local audit log) for full traceability and human review.

Multi-Hop Reasoning LLM Agents FastAPI / Next.js Observability / Audit

FinRL-X — XGBoost Selection + TD3 Allocation, Productionized

Extended and productionized FinRL-X (the open-source AI4Finance weight-centric quant framework), centering two strategies of our own: an XGBoost fundamental stock-selection model (quarterly, point-in-time, no-lookahead) and a TD3 deep-RL portfolio allocator, both emitting a shared target-weight vector. Built the deployment layer around them — live/paper execution on Alpaca (multi-account, pre-trade risk checks), a multi-source data pipeline (Yahoo / FMP / WRDS) with SQLite caching, and a one-command Dockerized path carrying identical weights from backtest to live. The ensemble was deployed to Alpaca paper trading at a 1.96 Sharpe.

XGBoost TD3 (Deep RL) FinRL-X Alpaca Execution

Tradfi-Agent — Autonomous Perpetual-Futures Trading

An autonomous trading agent for tokenized equities and ETFs on Binance USD-margined perpetual futures. A two-stage multi-hop reasoning loop first infers a macro regime from index ETFs, then fans out five parallel sector-level LLM calls conditioned on that regime to emit per-token long/short/hold decisions with confidence, volatility-aware take-profit/stop-loss, and funding-rate-adjusted entry thresholds. Orders run through a risk gate (exposure caps, spread guards, position sizing) and three-layer server-side exit protection over async REST and dual market/account WebSocket streams.
